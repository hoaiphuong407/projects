Matcha Cookies

Recipe from Dessert for Two

30 minutes to make (including prep and clean up)

10 cookies

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup, 1/3 cup, 1 tbsp, 1/4 tsp, 1 bowl, 1 whisk
  • 1 stick of unsalted butter
  • 2/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp matcha powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla

Steps

  1. Oven at 350 degrees. Bake for 10-11 minutes.
  2. Mix butter, sugar, egg, and vanilla.
  3. Mix flour, matcha, baking soda, and salt.
